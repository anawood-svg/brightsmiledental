Frequently Asked Questions

Find gentle answers to your concerns about insurance, pain management, and more.

We specialize in gentle care for anxious patients. We offer several levels of sedation, noise-canceling headphones, weighted blankets, and "The Wand" — a computer-controlled local anesthetic delivery system that is virtually painless. You're in complete control at every step.
We accept most major dental insurance plans including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, Guardian, and many more. Our billing team will verify your benefits before your visit, so there are no surprises. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients.
